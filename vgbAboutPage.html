MISSION

Vegan Bakery Hut aims to spread the compassionate vegan diet by producing bakery products by sourcing all plant based and low resources consuming ingredients, ultimately contributing to a thriving and a blissful planet.

Message from the Baker

Hello everybody! This is Sarita, the owner of Vegan Bakery Hut, and my warmest welcome to you all. To all the vegans or those who are in the process of being one, or maybe you are just enthusiastic of what vegan baked goods look and taste like, I thank you for your compassionate way of living and choices. I chose this cruelty free lifestyle back in 2011, and being a lover of baking, I have learnt and experimented so many recipes since. This platform is for me to share with you all these, as well as love and so much more. Cheers!

Why Vegan?

I believe the question should be, “why not"? With drastic changes in the climate unfolding right before our eyes, this is the high time for us to pause and think about how our actions and choices are contributing to the changing planet. We know it is important that we reuse and recycle as much as possible, but numerous studies have identified animal factories and production as the single most and highest contributor of greenhouse gases. This is something that we as human race cannot ignore and let continue due to our inactions. So, let’s make responsible and compassionate choices, and may our mother earth thrive! Peace to all the sentient beings.
